About Us

India's Textile Hub,
Your Trusted Partner

Welcome to SHIVAMM GARMENTS, a dedicated women's clothing manufacturer and wholesale supplier based in B-144, Global Textile Market, Surat, Gujarat — the fashion and textile capital of India.

We specialise exclusively in wholesale supply of premium women's garments, offering an extensive range of trendy and classic designs crafted with precision and care. Our mission is to empower retailers, boutique owners, and bulk buyers with high-quality fashion at highly competitive wholesale prices.

"Surat is India's unrivalled textile hub — and we bring you the very best from its heart."

With a sharp eye for the latest trends and a commitment to consistent quality, we regularly introduce new collections keeping your inventory fresh and your customers coming back. Every piece is made with meticulous quality control, ensuring that what reaches you is nothing short of premium.

Note: We deal exclusively in wholesale. Retail sales are not entertained.
